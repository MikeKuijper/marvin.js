Neural Networks without the headache


I know, Neural Networks are complicated and confusing. That's why Marvin.js was created from the ground up to be as intuitive as possible. That saves you some sleepless nights. You don't have to spend hours just to understand the basics of Neural Networks. If you know a little JavaScript, you know enough to use it.

Marvin.js is completely open-source and free to use for anyone, as long as you credit me. For more details about the license, click here.

I constantly update it and add new functionality to make it better. If you do manage to find a bug, glitch or mistake, you can add an issue on GitHub and I'll get to work on fixing it.
